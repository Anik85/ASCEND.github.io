About ASCEND

What is ASCEND?

ASCEND is an emerging StartUp Company that works tirelessly to unfold the inherent potentials of the students by enhancing their soft and hard skills and helping them with their academics. Recently, ASCEND was recognized as the one of the best promising StartUp ideas in an idea pitching competition conducted by the Bangladesh Hi-tech park authority. The Company (ASCEND) has got an office at Sheikh Kamal IT Incubation and Training center to conduct their Organizational activities.
